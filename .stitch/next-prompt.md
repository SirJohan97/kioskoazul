Full menu page for "El Capitán de la Playa" beachfront restaurant.

A comprehensive, interactive menu page organized by time of day using tabs:
- Desayunos (breakfast items)  
- Almuerzos (lunch specials)
- Cócteles (cocktails and beverages)
- Cenas Gourmet (gourmet dinner)

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, Mobile-first
- Theme: Dark, cinematic luxury — deep naval blues and night blacks
- Background: linear-gradient(160deg, #06121e, #0d1b36, #15102a)
- Primary Accent: Warm Sunset Gold (#F5A623) for CTAs and highlights
- Secondary Accent: Ocean Teal (#00B4D8)
- Text Primary: Cream White (#FFF8F0)
- Text Secondary: Soft Pearl (#D6CFC7)
- Cards: Glassmorphism (backdrop-filter blur, rgba backgrounds, subtle gold borders)
- Font: 'Outfit' headings, 'Inter' body
- Buttons: Pill-shaped with gold glow

**Page Structure:**
1. Sticky nav (same as homepage)
2. Page header — elegant title "Menú Completo" with eyebrow and subtitle
3. Category tabs: Desayunos | Almuerzos | Cócteles | Cenas
4. Tabbed menu grid — 2-3 columns of glassmorphism cards with dish name, description, price
5. Footer with WhatsApp CTA and nav links

# El Capitán de la Playa — Site Vision

## Section 1: Vision Statement

A premium, time-reactive single-page beachfront restaurant experience. The site dynamically adapts its background, mood, and menu recommendations to the user's local time of day — creating a truly alive, "fachero" digital presence that feels like the beach itself.

## Section 2: Brand Identity

- **Name:** El Capitán de la Playa
- **Cuisine:** Coastal Latin — fresh seafood, tropical cocktails, gourmet fusion
- **Vibe:** Premium beachfront, relaxed elegance, sun-soaked luxury
- **Audience:** Tourists, locals, couples, foodies seeking an immersive coastal dining experience
- **Colors:** Ocean blues, warm sandy golds, sunset magentas, deep indigos
- **Typography:** Modern, airy — think Inter, Outfit, or Playfair Display for headers
- **WhatsApp CTA:** https://wa.me/1234567890 (placeholder, update as needed)
